Description

This recipe follows the most popular lasgna recipe found online. It was created by a fellow named John Chandler. Unfortunately he passed away, but his love for cooking lives on in the form of recipes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasgna noodles
  • Ricotta, parmesan, and mozarella cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasgna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasgna rest before serving
